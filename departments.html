Project PHIN.See for yourself.

Project PHIN is to help orphan and semi-orphan hearing impaired children who were in need of educational facilities. As the children were impaired of hearing ability, all their learning is only by visual means. The orphanage needed boards for teaching, slates to practice, A4 size papers for conducting examinations etc. The Education team took the initiative to help them and provided them with the requirements.

Project Radha Krishna Balika Bhavan. Checkmate.

Project Radha Kishan Balika Bhavan is to help the orphan children and women in dire need of basic needs. The Orphanage is in the need of basic electronics like tube lights and sanitary napkins. We were told that the quality of the sanitary napkins given by other donors are the main cause of infections for children. So, we made sure that the problem is solved this time. Our women empowerment team resolved both the above mentioned issues, interacted with kids over there and talked about menstrual hygiene.
