Education

Education is the process of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, beliefs, and habits. Educational methods include teaching, training, and directed research.

Project PHIN.

Project PHIN is to help orphan and semi-orphan hearing impaired children who were in need of educational facilities. As the children were impaired of hearing ability, all their learning is only by visual means. The orphanage needed boards for teaching, slates to practice, A4 size papers for conducting examinations etc. The Education team took the initiative to help them and provided them with the requirements.
