SCV

Initially the group started with around 8 people in total. The events they did were not published and publicised properly, therefore not many people knew about the work we did and the reason we collected funds. Once this problem was analysed and understood we made sure that we correct our mistakes and improve ourselves in all the aspects we could. Every year has been a great learning and we are delighted to see more people joining us. The numbers have only been increasing each year i.e. starting from 8 people we became a group of 20 then 30 and today we are a group of 52 young budding leaders and future responsible citizens who not only pledge to serve the people in need but also to learn how to be compassionate towards fellow beings.

A life without a cause is a life without an effect!
